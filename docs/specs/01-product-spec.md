# Product Specification

## Objective
Modernize Teclab library operations through a scalable digital platform.

## Business Problems
- Manual spreadsheet processes
- Duplicate records
- Low inventory visibility
- Slow lending workflows

## Success Metrics
- 80% reduction in manual updates
- <2 min average loan transaction
- Real-time inventory accuracy > 98%

## Out of Scope
- Payment gateway
- Mobile native apps
- Third-party ERP integrations
